Estructura y registro · Structure & Register

El email formal exige saludo y despedida coherentes, tono impersonal sin contracciones y una estructura de párrafos cortos: apertura, motivo, desarrollo, petición, cierre. El registro vale el 25 % del módulo de Writing: dominarlo es la diferencia entre B1 y B2.

🎯Linguaskill: esta pestaña es la base de Writing Part 1 (email formal o semi-formal, 50 palabras). El registro y la coherencia entre saludo y despedida son criterios evaluados directamente.
👋
A. Saludos y despedidas · Greetings & sign-offs
Empareja siempre el saludo con la despedida correcta
Dear Sir/Madam, → Yours faithfully, no conoces el nombre · totalmente formal
Dear Mr/Ms [Surname], → Yours sincerely, conoces el apellido · estándar formal
To Whom It May Concern, → Yours faithfully, muy formal · impersonal · cartas oficiales
Dear Hiring Manager, / Dear Recruitment Team, → Yours sincerely, solicitudes de empleo sin nombre concreto
Dear Customer Services, / Dear Sales Department, → Yours faithfully, a un departamento
Dear [First name + Surname], → Kind regards, semi-formal · contactos profesionales
Hi [First name], → Best regards, / Many thanks, profesional pero cercano · solo si ya os tratáis así
Kind regards, / Best regards, / Regards, cierres semi-formales aceptables casi siempre
⚠️ Coma tras el saludo (uso británico): Dear Mr Smith, en US se admite también dos puntos en cartas comerciales

C. Estructura ideal · Ideal structure
Seis pasos que funcionan para cualquier email formal
1
Saludo · Greeting

Identifica al destinatario con un saludo coherente con la despedida que usarás al final.

Dear Sir/Madam, Dear Mr/Ms Smith, To Whom It May Concern,
2
Propósito · Opening line

Desde la primera línea explica claramente por qué escribes. Sé directo y específico.

I am writing to enquire about… I am writing with reference to… I am writing to complain about… I am writing to thank you for…
3
Desarrollo · Body

Aporta contexto, datos concretos, fechas o el problema. Un párrafo por idea, conectado con conectores formales.

Furthermore, In addition, However, Despite this,
4
Petición · Request / Action

Pide claramente lo que necesitas: información, acción, respuesta, refund o reschedule.

I would be grateful if you could… Could you please clarify…? I would appreciate it if you could…
5
Cierre cortés · Polite closing

Ofrece disponibilidad para seguir en contacto y agradece el tiempo del lector.

I look forward to hearing from you. Please do not hesitate to contact me. Thank you for your time and consideration.
6
Despedida + firma · Sign-off

Empareja la despedida con el saludo: Dear Sir/Madam → Yours faithfully; con nombre → Yours sincerely.

Yours faithfully, Yours sincerely, Kind regards,
📏 Writing P1: 50 palabras mínimo · 3–4 párrafos cortos · tono impersonal, sin contracciones · una sola idea por párrafo

D. Trampas para hispanohablantes
Errores que detecta el examinador en segundos
!
"Yours faithfully" SOLO tras "Dear Sir/Madam"

Si has empezado con Dear Sir/Madam, (no conoces el nombre), cierras con Yours faithfully,. Si has empezado con Dear Mr Smith, (sabes el nombre), cierras con Yours sincerely,. Mezclarlos es el error nº 1 — el examinador lo detecta inmediatamente.

!
"Dear Mr Smith" = Mr + APELLIDO, nunca + nombre

*Dear Mr John ✗ → Dear Mr Smith ✓ (con el apellido). Si solo conoces el nombre, usa Dear John (semi-formal) o Dear Sir/Madam (formal). Mr/Ms/Mrs/Dr siempre acompañan al apellido.

!
Nada de contracciones ni "Hi/Hello"

En formal escribe siempre la forma completa: I am writing, do not hesitate, cannot. Y nunca abras con Hi!, Hello! ni Hey: son saludos casuales que bajan el registro de toda la carta.

!
"I am writing to + INFINITIVO"

Calco frecuente del español: *I write you for tell that… ✗ → I am writing to inform you that… ✓. El presente continuo + to + infinitivo es la fórmula de apertura por excelencia. Nunca for + infinitivo.

!
"I would be grateful if you could + INFINITIVO"

Fórmula obligatoria para peticiones formales. *I would grateful that you send me ✗ → I would be grateful if you could send me… ✓. Y siempre con could tras if (no would), seguido de infinitivo sin to.

!
"Please find attached" — no "I attach you"

Calco: *I attach you my CV ✗ → Please find attached my CV ✓ · o I have attached my CV for your reference. El verbo attach no toma persona como objeto en este contexto.

!
"I look forward to + GERUNDIO"

Aquí to es preposición, no parte del infinitivo: *I look forward to hear from you ✗ → I look forward to hearing from you ✓ · … to receiving your reply · … to meeting you next week. Verbo en -ing, sin excepción.

!
Coma tras el saludo (uso británico)

Dear Mr Smith, con coma. En UK la coma es la norma; en US se admite también dos puntos (Dear Mr Smith:) en cartas comerciales. En Linguaskill, sigue la convención británica por defecto.

!
Una idea por párrafo, sin muros de texto

En la Writing P1, divide en 3–4 párrafos cortos: apertura + motivo, desarrollo, petición/acción, cierre + despedida. Un muro de texto sin saltos baja la nota de Coherence & Cohesion.

Solicitud y consulta · Request & Enquiry

Email formal para pedir información, solicitar materiales, aplicar a un puesto o reservar un servicio. La fórmula clave es I am writing to enquire about / request… seguida de la petición concreta con I would be grateful if you could…

🎯Linguaskill: uno de los tres propósitos más frecuentes de Writing Part 1. Variar entre enquire / request / would appreciate / would be grateful demuestra rango lexical y sube el descriptor.
⭐
A. Frases clave · Key phrases
Fórmulas para abrir, pedir y cerrar una solicitud
I am writing to enquire about… apertura · consulta
I am writing to request further information regarding… apertura · solicitud formal
I am writing with reference to your advertisement / website / email of [date]… apertura · respuesta a algo previo
I would like to apply for the position of… / the course advertised… apertura · aplicación
I would be grateful if you could send me / provide / confirm… petición estándar
I would appreciate it if you could clarify whether… petición + duda concreta
Could you please let me know / inform me of…? petición directa pero cortés
I would also like to know whether… / how much… / when… añadir una segunda pregunta
Please find attached my CV / a copy of my passport / further documentation. adjuntos
I look forward to hearing from you / to your reply. cierre · espera respuesta
Thank you in advance for your assistance. cierre · cortesía

E. Modelo · Sample emails
Dos modelos completos con la estructura resaltada

Model 1 · Enquiry about a summer English course

Subject: Enquiry — Summer Intensive English Course · ~150 words

Dear Sir/Madam,

OpeningI am writing to enquire about the intensive English course advertised on your website for the summer term.

BodyI am currently preparing for the Linguaskill B2 exam in August, and I would like to know whether your course covers exam-style writing and speaking practice. I would also be grateful if you could confirm the maximum class size and the qualifications of the teachers.

RequestCould you please send me a detailed brochure with the full timetable, the fees and the registration deadline? I would appreciate it if you could also indicate whether accommodation can be arranged through your school.

ClosingI look forward to hearing from you at your earliest convenience.

Yours faithfully,
Carlos Vega

Model 2 · Application for a part-time job

Subject: Application — Part-time marketing assistant · ~155 words

Dear Ms Reynolds,

OpeningI am writing with reference to your recent advertisement on LinkedIn for the position of part-time marketing assistant at Brightside Agency.

ProfileI am in my final year of a Business Administration degree at the University of Valencia and I have completed two summer internships in social media management. I am proficient in English, Spanish and Catalan, and familiar with Canva, Mailchimp and Google Analytics.

SuitabilityMy flexible schedule would allow me to begin immediately and commit to the twenty hours per week mentioned in the listing. Please find attached my CV and two references for your consideration.

ClosingI would welcome the opportunity to discuss my application further at your convenience. I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours sincerely,
Andrés Molina

Queja · Complaint

Email formal para quejarse de un producto defectuoso, un servicio insatisfactorio o un incidente. Mantén el tono firme pero cortés: nunca insultos ni exclamaciones. La estructura típica es problema → consecuencia → petición (reembolso, reemplazo, disculpa) → cierre con expectativa de respuesta.

🎯Linguaskill: el tono mesurado se valora explícitamente. I am extremely disappointed ✓ ; This is unacceptable!! ✗. La queja eficaz combina precisión factual con cortesía sostenida.
⭐
A. Frases clave · Key phrases
Fórmulas para quejarse con firmeza y mantener el registro
I am writing to complain about… apertura directa
I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with… apertura formal
I am writing to draw your attention to… apertura · llamar la atención
I regret to inform you that… tono cortés pero firme
I am extremely disappointed with the service / product I received… expresar disgusto sin gritar
Unfortunately, the service did not meet my expectations for the following reasons:… introducir lista de problemas
To make matters worse, nobody responded to my calls / emails. añadir agravante
As a result, I had to… / I was unable to… consecuencia del problema
I would like to request a full refund / a replacement / appropriate compensation. petición concreta
I would be grateful if you could look into this matter and provide an explanation. petición de revisión
I trust you will resolve this matter at your earliest convenience. cierre con expectativa
Should this issue not be resolved, I will have no option but to take further action. cierre firme · usar con moderación
I look forward to your prompt response. cierre estándar de queja

Model 1 · Complaint about a hotel stay

Subject: Disappointing stay at the Grand Plaza Hotel · ~160 words

Dear Mr Thompson,

OpeningI am writing to express my dissatisfaction with my recent stay at the Grand Plaza Hotel from 12 to 15 March.

ProblemUpon arrival, I was informed that my pre-booked sea-view room was unavailable, and I was relocated to a much smaller room overlooking the car park. Furthermore, the air conditioning was faulty throughout my stay, despite three separate requests for maintenance. To make matters worse, the breakfast service ended thirty minutes earlier than advertised on your website.

RequestI would be grateful if you could provide a full explanation regarding these issues and offer appropriate compensation for the inconvenience caused. I trust you will look into this matter at your earliest convenience.

ClosingI look forward to your prompt response.

Yours sincerely,
Marta García

Model 2 · Refund request for a defective product

Subject: Refund request — order #BX-48329 · ~155 words

Dear Customer Services,

OpeningI am writing to complain about my recent order (number #BX-48329), placed on 3 February through your online store.

ProblemThe package arrived two weeks later than the estimated delivery date, and upon opening it I discovered that one of the items — a pair of leather boots — was clearly defective, with a torn seam and a broken zip. Despite contacting your support team three times by phone, I have not received any response or follow-up.

RequestIn view of these circumstances, I would be grateful if you could process a full refund for the defective item, including the original delivery charge. I would also appreciate it if you could clarify why my previous enquiries went unanswered.

ClosingI trust this matter will be resolved promptly.

Yours faithfully,
Tomás Rivera

Agradecimiento y recomendación · Thanks & Recommendation

Email formal para agradecer un favor, una entrevista o un servicio, o para recomendar a alguien para un puesto, una beca o un programa. El tono es positivo y específico: nombra el motivo concreto del agradecimiento o las cualidades que justifican la recomendación.

🎯Linguaskill: propósito tercero más frecuente en Writing P1. Concreta siempre el motivo (thank you for arranging…, I recommend her because…) — el agradecimiento o la recomendación genéricos puntúan menos.

⭐
A. Frases clave · Key phrases
Fórmulas para agradecer o recomendar con registro formal
I am writing to thank you for… apertura · agradecer
I would like to express my sincere gratitude for… apertura formal · gratitud
I am most grateful for your time / your assistance / your prompt reply. gratitud concreta
Your support was greatly appreciated and made a significant difference. enfatizar impacto
Thank you for your prompt response to my recent enquiry. cortesía estándar
I am writing to recommend [Name] for the position of… / for the [Programme] at… apertura · recomendación
It is my pleasure to recommend [Name], whom I have known for… apertura formal · recomendación
During his/her time with us, [Name] consistently demonstrated… desarrollo · cualidades
I have no hesitation in recommending [Name] for this role. apoyo sin reservas
I am confident that [Name] would be an excellent addition to your team. cierre · proyección positiva
Should you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact me. cierre · disponibilidad
Once again, thank you for your time and consideration. cierre cortés

Model 2 · Recommendation letter for a former student

Subject: Recommendation — Laura Beltrán, MA in International Relations · ~160 words

Dear Admissions Committee,

OpeningIt is my pleasure to recommend Ms Laura Beltrán for admission to the Master's programme in International Relations at the University of Edinburgh.

BackgroundI taught Ms Beltrán Comparative Politics during her third year and supervised her dissertation on European migration policy. Throughout her degree, she consistently demonstrated outstanding analytical skills, intellectual curiosity and the ability to engage critically with complex texts.

SuitabilityI have no hesitation in recommending her for your programme, as her research interests align closely with your department's focus on policy analysis. I am confident that she would contribute significantly to academic discussions and the wider student community.

ClosingShould you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Yours faithfully,
Prof. James Hughes
